About VJDQ

Welcome to our Data Science Club, the premier hub for data science enthusiasts at our college. Established with the vision of driving innovation and collaboration, our club serves as the central point for all data science-related activities on campus.

We provide comprehensive guidance on projects, offer certifications for both students and faculty, and keep our members informed about the latest industry trends and real-world applications of data science.
